BIG MAC BURRITOS

Ingredients

  • 1kg of ground beef
  • 10 slices of cheddar
  • 5 cups of shredded american lettuce
  • 2 onions, fine diced
  • 10 tortillas (you can check the recipe for homemade tortillas here)
  • big mac sauce

Ingredients for the sauce

  • 8 tsp mayonnaise
  • 4 tsp ketchup
  • 3 tsp yellow mustard
  • 5 tsp vinegar
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp sweet paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • ⅔ cup of finely minced pickles
  • salt and pepper to the taste

(pro tip: add a touch of sugar to give it a bittersweet flavor)
