THE BIG MAC SECRET SAUCE

The secret to the legendary Big Mac sauce isn't ketchup (a common misconception!)—it's actually a mayonnaise-based sauce with a tangy, sweet, and slightly savory kick. Here is the breakdown of what makes up that famous "Special Sauce" and how to make it at home:

The Flavor Profile

Big Mac sauce is creamy, tangy, and sweet, with a distinct hit of pickle relish and vinegar. It gets its iconic yellowish-orange hue from a blend of turmeric and paprika, not from tomato paste or ketchup.

Ingredients for the sauce

  • 8 tsp mayonnaise
  • 4 tsp ketchup
  • 3 tsp yellow mustard
  • 5 tsp vinegar
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp sweet paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • ⅔ cup of finely minced pickles
  • salt and pepper to the taste

    • (pro tip: add a touch of sugar to give it a bittersweet flavor)

    mix everything in your favorite bowl.

    fry some french potatoes, dip and enjoy!
