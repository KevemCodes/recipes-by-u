BIG MAC BURRITOS

The Ultimate Fusion: Big Mac Burritos

If you love the iconic flavors of a Big Mac but sometimes want something a bit more portable (and let’s face it, less messy), this is about to become your new favorite meal. We are taking everything that makes the classic fast-food burger great and rolling it up into a warm, toasted flour tortilla.

You get the seasoned ground beef, the melted cheese, the crunch of the lettuce and pickles, and most importantly, that legendary special sauce. It’s fresh, it’s incredibly easy to make at home, and it hits the spot every single time.

Why This Recipe Just Works:

  • That Sauce, But Better: We cracked the code on the secret sauce using simple pantry staples.
  • Zero Soggy Buns: Rolling everything into a tortilla keeps the ingredients packed tight, so you get the perfect bite every time without the mess.
  • Under 30 Minutes: It’s actually faster than driving to the drive-thru, paying, and waiting in line.

Whether you're looking for a fun Friday night dinner or a quick meal prep idea that doesn't taste boring, these burritos are an absolute game-changer.

Ingredients

  • garlic
  • 1kg of ground beef
  • 10 slices of cheddar
  • 5 cups of shredded american lettuce
  • 2 onions, fine diced
  • 10 tortillas (you can check the recipe for homemade tortillas here)
  • big mac sauce
  • seasonings

Instructions

1. cooking the beef

  1. in a high heat frying pan put a drizzle of oil
  2. put about 2 medium sized cloves of garlic
  3. add about 1 medium sized onion fine diced

    4. mix until the garlic gets golden and the onion starts to get transparent

  4. throw in the beef and cook until it browns nicely
  5. salt and pepper to the taste
  6. put it aside

2. make a very fine chopped salad (we recommend american lettuce, tomatoes, onions and a little bit of lemon juice.)

3. prepare the burritos

(note: spread all the ingredients in a horizontal line on the lower part of the burrito)

  1. spread the sauce
  2. put the ground beef on top of the sauce
  3. sprinkle the salad
  4. sprinkle the cheese

4. rolling the burritos

5. sealing

  1. heat a frying pan
  2. put the rolled burritos on the pan with the seal down
  3. 15 seconds each side

now put it on a plate and enjoy!
